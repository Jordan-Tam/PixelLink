About PixelLink

PixelLink is a website designed to help gamers find friends and hopefully future teammates for games they play and any future games they may play!

  • Add your favorite games to your profile!
  • Browse through the game list for new games!
  • View the profiles and games list of your friends!
  • Make new friends!
  • Chat with friends!
